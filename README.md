# Steth2Tech

Clinical AI and Biomedical Data Science Portfolio of Dr. Sushant Tapase

## Overview

Steth2Tech is a physician-led portfolio showcasing projects at the intersection of clinical medicine, biomedical data science, healthcare analytics, and machine learning.

The platform documents the transition from clinical practice to applied healthcare data science through real-world projects, case studies, and technical implementations.

Website: https://steth2tech.com

## Background

Dr. Sushant Tapase is an MBBS physician with experience in clinical research, healthcare analytics, clinical documentation review, and biomedical data science.

Current academic pursuits include:

* M.Tech in Biomedical Data Science
* MBA in Healthcare and Hospital Management

## Featured Projects

### Healthcare Revenue Intelligence Platform

Executive-grade healthcare analytics platform for DRG benchmarking, LOS intelligence, coding opportunity detection, and revenue leakage mitigation.

### Oncology Survival Modeling

Survival analysis pipeline integrating oncology registry and clinical trial data using Cox Proportional Hazards and Random Survival Forest models.

### HCC Extractor

Rule-based clinical NLP system for extracting ICD-10 diagnoses and mapping them to Hierarchical Condition Categories (HCCs).

### DriftGuard

Monitoring framework for detecting distributional drift in anesthesia-related physiological signals.

## Purpose

The objective of Steth2Tech is to demonstrate clinically grounded approaches to healthcare AI, emphasizing practical implementation, reproducibility, and real-world relevance.
